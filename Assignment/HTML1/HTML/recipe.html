A simple Oreo cake recipe

Ingredients required

  • A pack of oreo biscuits
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tb of sugar
  • A pinch of baking soda

Process to make a oreo cake

  1. Take a packet of oreo biscuits. Gring the biscuits in a mixer.
  2. After the biscuits are grind properly. Add milk and grind again.
  3. After that add sugar. And a pinch of baking soda.
  4. Take the mixture into your baking tray.
  5. Preheat the oven at 180c, for 2 minutes. Place the cake in the oven and bake for 5 minutes.
  6. Your oreo cake is ready to eat. You can put ganache or icing for decorating the cake
  7. Apply some oil to the mixer and baking tray to avoid stickiness
